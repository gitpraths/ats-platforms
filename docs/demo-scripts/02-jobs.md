## Script

### [0:00–0:30] Jobs List Overview
**ACTION:** Show the Jobs list page with all jobs visible.

**NARRATE:**
> "The Jobs module is the heart of the platform.
> Here we can see every role across the organisation — the job title, department,
> location, how many applications each role has received, and its current status."

**ACTION:** Point to the status badges (Draft, Published, Archived).

**NARRATE:**
> "Jobs move through three states: Draft while you're writing them,
> Published when they're live and accepting applications,
> and Archived when the role is closed."

---

### [0:30–1:30] Create a New Job
**ACTION:** Click **New Job** or **+ Create Job** button.

**NARRATE:**
> "Let me show you how to create a new job. The creation wizard has four steps."

**ACTION:** Step 1 — Basics. Fill in:
- Title: `Retail Team Member`
- Department: select `Retail Operations`
- Location: select `Melbourne CBD`
- Employment Type: `Full Time`
- Salary: `$55,000 – $60,000`

**NARRATE:**
> "Step one covers the basics — job title, department, location, employment type, and salary range."

**ACTION:** Click **Next**.

**NARRATE:**
> "Step two is the job description. Notice the AI button — we'll cover that in the AI features video.
> For now I'll paste in a description."

**ACTION:** Type a short description. Click **Next**.

**NARRATE:**
> "Step three captures the requirements — skills and qualifications needed for the role."

**ACTION:** Add 2 requirements. Click **Next**.

**NARRATE:**
> "Step four assigns recruiters to this job. Recruiters assigned here will see these
> applications on their Hiring Board."

**ACTION:** Assign Emma Wilson. Click **Create Job**.

**NARRATE:**
> "The job is created in Draft status and we land on the Job Detail page."

---

### [1:30–2:30] Job Detail Page
**ACTION:** Show the Job Detail page.

**NARRATE:**
> "The Job Detail page shows everything about a role in one place.
> The header has the job title, status badge, department, location, and salary.
> Below that we have the full description and requirements."

**ACTION:** Scroll down to show the Applications section and Activity Log.

**NARRATE:**
> "Applications linked to this job appear here with their current stage.
> And every change to the job — status updates, edits, recruiter assignments —
> is recorded in the Activity Log for full audit trail."

---

### [2:30–3:30] Status Transitions
**ACTION:** Find the status dropdown or button on the job detail.

**NARRATE:**
> "To publish a job and make it live, we change the status here."

**ACTION:** Change status from Draft to Published.

**NARRATE:**
> "The job is now Published. Candidates can be assigned to it and applications can flow in.
> When a role is filled or no longer needed, we archive it."

**ACTION:** Change status to Archived, then back to Published.

**NARRATE:**
> "Notice the status badge updates instantly.
> The system prevents publishing a job without a title and department —
> these business rules are enforced at the API level."

---

### [3:30–4:15] Edit a Job
**ACTION:** Click the **Edit** button.

**NARRATE:**
> "Clicking Edit opens the full edit form where we can update any field —
> description, requirements, salary range, employment type."

**ACTION:** Change the salary field. Click **Save**.

**NARRATE:**
> "Changes are saved immediately and reflected across the platform.
> The activity log captures who made the change and when."

---

### [4:15–5:00] Recruiter Assignment
**ACTION:** Scroll to or navigate to the Recruiters section on the job.

**NARRATE:**
> "Recruiter assignment determines who owns this job.
> Assigned recruiters see these applications on their personal Hiring Board,
> receive notifications, and can update application stages.
> This allows a team leader to distribute workload across the team clearly."

**ACTION:** Show the recruiter list, then navigate back to Jobs list.

**NARRATE:**
> "In the next video we'll look at the Hiring Board where all these applications
> come together in a visual Kanban workflow."

**ACTION:** Stop recording.
