### [3:30–4:15] Deactivate an Employer
**ACTION:** Show the Deactivate button on an employer.

**NARRATE:**
> "If a business relationship ends, we deactivate the employer.
> This hides them from new job and placement forms but preserves all historical records.
> Similar to providers, the system blocks deactivation if there are still open jobs
> linked to this employer — those need to be archived first."

---

### [4:15–5:00] Employer vs Provider Relationship
**ACTION:** Show the Employers list side by side with the Providers list in two tabs.

**NARRATE:**
> "To summarise the data model clearly:
> Providers support candidates — they are employment service organisations
> that prepare candidates for work.
> Employers hire candidates — they are the businesses that offer jobs.
> A candidate is linked to one provider and can be placed with many employers over time.
> This distinction maps directly to the DES employment services model
> used across Australia."

**NARRATE:**
> "In the next video we'll look at Reports — where all this data
> comes together into insights and exports."
