# Video 1 — Dashboard
**Duration:** ~5 minutes
**Login as:** admin@myats.dev / password123

---

## Pre-recording Checklist
- [ ] App is running (frontend + backend)
- [ ] Browser at http://localhost:5173
- [ ] Logged out (or private/incognito window)

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:30] Introduction
**ACTION:** Show the login page.

**NARRATE:**
> "Welcome to the ATS Platform — a full recruitment management system built for employment service providers.
> Let me log in as an administrator to walk you through the dashboard."

**ACTION:** Type `admin@myats.dev`, type `password123`, click **Log In**.

---

### [0:30–1:30] Top Stats Row
**ACTION:** The dashboard loads. Pause on the top row of 4 stat cards.

**NARRATE:**
> "The dashboard gives us a live snapshot of the entire recruitment operation.
> Up here we have our four core metrics —
> Open Jobs, Active Applications, people Hired This Month, and Total Candidates in the system."

**ACTION:** Slowly move your mouse over each card.

**NARRATE:**
> "Each card is clickable and takes you directly to that section. Every number you see here
> is pulled in real time from the database — no manual refresh needed."

---

### [1:30–2:15] Placements & Provider Stats Row
**ACTION:** Scroll down slightly to show the second row of cards.

**NARRATE:**
> "Below that we have our placement and provider metrics.
> Total Placements, Placements This Month, Active Providers, and Active Employers.
> This second row is specific to the Employment Services model —
> tracking how many candidates have been placed with employers through our provider network."

---

### [2:15–2:45] Welfare Alert Banner
**ACTION:** Point to the yellow warning banner (if overdue welfare checks exist).

**NARRATE:**
> "This amber alert banner appears automatically when welfare checks are overdue.
> Welfare checks are scheduled follow-ups after a candidate is placed — at day 1, week 1,
> one month, three months, and six months. If any are overdue, the system flags it here
> so nothing falls through the cracks. Clicking it takes you straight to the Placements page."

---

### [2:45–3:30] Charts — Jobs by Status & Pipeline
**ACTION:** Scroll to the two charts side by side.

**NARRATE:**
> "The 'Jobs by Status' bar chart shows how many roles are in Draft, Published, or Archived state.
> This helps managers see at a glance how active the recruitment pipeline is."

**ACTION:** Point to the right chart — Application Pipeline.

**NARRATE:**
> "The Application Pipeline funnel shows every application broken down by stage —
> Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer, Hired, and Rejected.
> You can immediately see where candidates are clustering and if any stage is a bottleneck."

---

### [3:30–4:15] Placement Summary
**ACTION:** Scroll to the Placement Summary panel.

**NARRATE:**
> "The Placement Summary gives us three key numbers side by side —
> Total Placements, how many have been Confirmed by the Employer,
> and Overdue Welfare Checks highlighted in amber if any exist.
> This single panel tells a team leader everything they need to know about placement health."

---

### [4:15–5:00] Recent Applications
**ACTION:** Scroll to the Recent Applications list at the bottom.

**NARRATE:**
> "Finally, the Recent Applications section shows the last ten applications
> across all jobs in real time. You can see the candidate name, which job they applied for,
> their current pipeline stage as a colour-coded badge, and how long ago they applied.
> The 'View board' link takes you to the full Kanban Hiring Board."

**ACTION:** Click **View board** link.

**NARRATE:**
> "In the next video we'll look at the Jobs module and how to create and manage job postings."

**ACTION:** Stop recording.
