## Script

### [0:00–0:30] Providers List
**ACTION:** Show the Providers list page — table with 5 providers.

> "Providers are the employment service organisations — like MAX Employment,
> atWork Australia, and APM Employment Services — that work with candidates

> how many active candidates they're supporting, and whether they're active."

**NARRATE:**

**ACTION:** Point to the stats row at the top (total candidates, active placements, etc.).

**NARRATE:**

### [2:45–3:30] Deactivate a Provider
**ACTION:** Show the Deactivate button (or toggle) on the provider detail.

**NARRATE:**
> "If a provider contract ends, we can deactivate them.
> Deactivated providers no longer appear in dropdown selectors when adding candidates,
> but their historical data is preserved.
> The system will block deactivation if the provider still has active candidates —
> you'd need to reassign those candidates first."

**ACTION:** Log out. Log in as `rachel@maxemployment.com.au` / `password123`.
