### [1:00–2:15] Candidate Profile — Personal & Work Details
**ACTION:** Show the top of the candidate profile — name, contact info, location.

**NARRATE:**
> "The candidate profile brings together everything we know about this person.
> At the top we have their contact details — email, phone, suburb, state, postcode —
> and their current work status."

**ACTION:** Point to the work status badge (e.g. "Employed - Part Time").

**NARRATE:**
> "Work status is important for employment service reporting.
> It tracks whether a candidate is job-seeking, employed part-time,
> casually, or in supported employment."

**ACTION:** Scroll down to the Provider & Support section.

**NARRATE:**
> "This section shows which Provider is supporting this candidate —
> in this case DHL Supply Chain through MAX Employment —
> their benchmark hours per week, and the type of role they're interested in.
> These fields come directly from the DES reporting requirements."

---

### [2:15–3:00] Application History
**ACTION:** Scroll to the Applications section on the profile.

**NARRATE:**
> "Every job application this candidate has ever had is listed here —
> the job title, company, which stage they reached, and when they applied.
> This gives recruiters and providers complete visibility of a candidate's history
> without needing to search across multiple jobs."
