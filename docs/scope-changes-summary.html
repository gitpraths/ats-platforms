Points 1, 5, 6 & Reports — Already Built Pre-existing

Point 1 — Super Admin / Staff Logins

5 roles: admin, recruiter_admin, recruiter, hiring_manager, provider. JWT authentication. AdminUsers page for user management. Provider users linked to their organisation.

Point 5 — Employer Details

Full Employers module: list, detail, create, and edit pages. Fields: name, industry, website, contact details, address. Shows open job count and placement count.

Point 6 — Placement Details

Placements module with one-click placement from application. Welfare checks at Day 1, Week 1, Month 1, 3, and 6. Automated email confirmation sent to employer on placement.

Reports

Reports page with 3 tabs: Provider Report (placement rates by provider), Placement Tracking (welfare check status), Staff Report (jobs and placements per staff member). CSV export on all tabs.
